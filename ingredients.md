DRY INGREDIENTS:

-2 tablespoons flour
-1 tablespoon sugar
-1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder (if you are using sweetened cocoa powder, then use less sugar)
-one fourth of a teaspoon baking powder

WET INGREDIENTS:

-1 tablespoon oil
-2 tablespoon milk
 
 OPTIONAL:

 -1 tbsp nutella 
 -sprinkles
 -powdered sugar
